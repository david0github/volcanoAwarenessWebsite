Marketing Plan

In order to raise awareness for volcanoes, Joseph Esposito devised a marketing plan in order to bring attention to this matter and try to make sure people stay safe. As a Marketing Major, it is Joseph job to devise a plan to get the people the message that we need and to figure out all of the little details such as cost.

Identifying a Target Audience

Narrowing down a target audience to create awareness for volcanoes wasn't exactly easy. However, as stated previously in this presentation some of the people who are generally threatened by a volcano are:

  • the local residents
  • the tourists
  • the volcanologists
  • the members of the media
These four locations all have over 100 volcanoes.

Marketing Goals

By running our campaign to raise awareness for volcanoes, we want to accomplish a few things:

  • Help everyone have a plan before the volcano erupts
  • Know what to do/where to find shelter when the volcano erupts.
  • We would like to see a 10% (54 people) decrease in the amount of the deaths per year from volcanoes.

Marketing Communication Strategy/Tactics

Tactics we will use in order to get out message to the people in the specific areas we are targeting:

  • Tevevision Ads
  • Internet Merketing (Social Media)
  • Newspaper Ads
We will be getting this message to not only poeple who leave in these high volcanic areas, but also people such as tourists and media who plan on visiting them.
