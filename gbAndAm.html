Designing the Campaign

We want to create awareness for the people living around active volcanoes. As a graphic design major my role is to create the style guide of the marketing campaign and design the website. After understanding the purpose, conduct research to increase knowledge about the topic and why it is important.

New York Post

Volcanoes killed 540 people per year. Local Residents are the most frequently killed, but tourists, volcanologoists and members of the media are also identified as common victims. There are 1,508 active volcanoes located in 86 countries and additional terrtories worldwide.

Target Market

Clearly want to prevent deaths and warn people about active volcanoes. Our target audience would be:

  • People would live near active volcanoes
  • Tourist going to places with active volcanoes
  • News and media people going to active volcano areas

Actual Design

When Designing for important information:

  • Fonts should be easy to read
  • Bright colors
  • Clear heirarchy of information

The color theme and dots should also match the design of the website for a clear understanding that the two are related. After creating clear design for the campaign the next step is the market the information.
